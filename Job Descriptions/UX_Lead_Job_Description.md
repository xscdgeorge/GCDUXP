# UX Lead
**Location:** Bellandur, Bangalore

## Company & Team Overview
HP aims to create technology that makes life better for everyone, everywhere. Combining the energy of a startup with the resources of a Fortune 100 corporation, we continue an 80+ year legacy of innovation. The India & Growing Market Development Center (IDC) is pivotal to our future, focusing on high-growth regions. Our mission is to develop products and technology solutions tailored specifically "for India and in India."

## Position Overview
We are seeking a visionary UX Lead to manage our overall user experience design strategy and drive the software experience of an exciting new hardware product. In this leadership role, you will guide teams of experienced designers to craft cohesive digital experiences that blend aesthetics with functionality. You will align localized solutions with HP's global guidelines, assign projects, manage resources, and foster a culture of creativity, innovation, and design excellence.

## Key Responsibilities
* **Strategic Leadership:** Provide direct and ongoing leadership for an organization of managers and individual contributors. Define future design visions that balance user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility while utilizing our core design system.
* **End-to-End Design:** Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product management, engineering, marketing) to design intuitive software interfaces for the Android ecosystem that align with global standards.
* **Program & Resource Management:** Oversee headcount, budgets, schedules, and deliverables for multiple projects. Ensure timely, high-quality execution in accordance with organizational roadmaps.
* **Stakeholder & Vendor Collaboration:** Engage with technical leaders, executives, ISVs, and marketing teams to communicate status, refine requirements, and influence strategic decisions. Manage relationships with outsourced partners, ensuring quality and seamless integration of white-labeled solutions.
* **People Management:** Provide direct people-care, including hiring, performance planning, coaching, and career development.
* **Process Improvement:** Proactively identify opportunities for process optimization, cost reduction, and adoption of best-in-class UX practices across the organization.

## Key Qualifications & Education
* **Education:** Bachelor’s, Master’s, or Graduate Degree in Product Design, Interaction Design, Human Factors Engineering, Social Sciences research, or a related discipline.
* **Experience:** **14+ years** of extensive experience in UI/UX design and leadership, encompassing team management and hands-on design expertise within the Android ecosystem.
* **Design Ecosystems:** Proven ability to integrate global design guidelines into localized projects and a deep understanding of hardware/software ecosystem principles.
* **Tools:** Proficiency in Figma, InVision, Adobe XD, or Sketch. Familiarity with motion design and sound integration tools is highly advantageous.
* **Certification (Preferred):** Certified User Experience Professional (CUXP).
